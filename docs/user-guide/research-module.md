# Research Module Guide

**Generate deep research prompts** — Validate assumptions before building.

---

## What It Does

The Research module:
- Generates research prompts for AI research tools
- Helps you gather market intelligence
- Identifies competitive landscape
- Recommends technical approaches
- Stores research results in Vault

---

## When to Use It

Use Research **after completing your Brief** and before Design. This ensures:
- You validate assumptions early
- You gather relevant market data
- You identify technical blind spots
- Your design decisions are informed

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Select Context Nodes

Choose what context to include:
- ☑️ **Brief** — Your problem statement and users
- ☐ **Tech Stack** — Your technology choices
- ☐ **User Personas** — Detailed user profiles

**Recommendation:** Always include Brief context.

### 2. Generate Research Prompt

Click **"Generate Research Prompt"**

The AI generates a comprehensive research prompt covering:
- Market & audience analysis
- Competitive landscape
- Technology recommendations
- Design patterns
- Prompt engineering best practices

### 3. Copy the Prompt

Click **"Copy"** to copy the generated prompt.

### 4. Paste into Research Tool

Use with:
- **Perplexity Deep Research** — Best for technical research
- **Gemini Deep Research** — Best for market analysis
- **ChatGPT Deep Research** — Best for competitive analysis

### 5. Upload Results

After research completes:
1. Save the research report (PDF or copy to MD)
2. Go to **Vault** tab
3. Upload the file
4. Mark as **"Active Context"**

---

## Tips & Best Practices

### Read the Full Research

Don't skim. Look for:
- Market size numbers (TAM, SAM, SOM)
- Direct competitors you didn't know about
- Technical recommendations specific to your stack
- Design patterns from similar successful apps

### Ask Follow-Up Questions

After initial research:
- "What are the top 3 technical risks for this approach?"
- "Show me 5 similar apps and how they solve [X]"
- "What's the optimal database schema for [use case]?"

### Save Everything to Vault

Research reports are gold:
- Reference during design decisions
- Include in PRD generation
- Share with team members
- Revisit during build phase

---

## Common Issues

### "The research is too generic"

**Solution:**
- Include more context in your Brief
- Select more context nodes
- Add follow-up questions to the research tool

### "I don't have access to Deep Research tools"

**Solution:**
- Use standard Perplexity/Gemini/ChatGPT
- Break the research prompt into smaller questions
- Research one topic at a time
